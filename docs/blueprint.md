# **App Name**: Cartera Website

## Core Features:

- Terms and Conditions Display: Display the terms and conditions in a readable format.
- Privacy Policy Display: Display the privacy policy in a readable format, including the specific privacy policy provided by the user, with organization name (UqabMedia) and contact email (media.uqab@gmail.com) clearly indicated.
- Contact Form: A contact form where users can submit inquiries related to privacy or terms, which then sends an email to media.uqab@gmail.com.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Deep blue (#3F51B5) to evoke trust and security.
- Background color: Light blue (#E8EAF6), a very desaturated shade of the primary color to ensure readability.
- Accent color: Yellow (#FFEB3B), an analogous color providing a warm and inviting feel while highlighting key elements.
- Body and headline font: 'Inter', a sans-serif font known for its clarity and readability in both headers and body text.
- Clean and structured layout for easy navigation and readability of legal documents.
- Use simple and clear icons to represent sections like 'Terms' and 'Privacy'.
- Subtle transition animations for a smooth user experience.
